FAQ

Common questions

What trades is Wrench built for? +

Wrench is designed for any field service or trades business — plumbers, HVAC technicians, electricians, landscapers, general contractors, pest control, cleaning services, and more. If your work involves scheduling jobs, sending quotes, and collecting payments from customers, Wrench was built for you.

Is $25/month really all-in? +

Yes — $25/month gets you every feature Wrench offers. No per-user fees, no add-ons, no surprise charges. Quotes, job scheduling, invoicing, SMS automations, team management, inventory tracking, and review requests are all included. We cut the bloat, not the features.

How does Wrench compare to other field service software? +

Most field service management platforms charge $49–$250/month and are designed for larger companies with dedicated office staff. Wrench is purpose-built for solo operators and small crews — the owner who's also in the field every day. You get the same core features at a fraction of the cost, with a mobile-first experience that actually makes sense on a phone.

Can I manage a team of technicians? +

Yes. Wrench supports up to 10 technicians on one account. You can assign jobs, set permissions per team member, track performance, and even send shoutouts to recognize great work. Each technician gets their own dashboard showing their scheduled jobs for the day.

Do I need a computer or does it work on my phone? +

Wrench is fully mobile — no app download required, just open it in your browser. It's designed specifically for use on a phone while you're on the job. You can create a quote, schedule a job, send an invoice, and request a Google review all from your truck between stops.
