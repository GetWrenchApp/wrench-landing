Text Messaging Consent & Policy

How Wrench sends SMS messages to customers on behalf of independent contractors.

📅 Last updated: February 2026
✅
How Consent Is Obtained
Opt-in process

Wrench is a job management platform used by independent handymen and solo service contractors. Customers provide their mobile phone number directly to the contractor when requesting a quote, scheduling a job, or engaging services.

By providing their phone number, the customer consents to receive transactional SMS messages related to their service job — including quotes, appointment confirmations, invoices, and follow-up messages. No phone number is collected or used without direct customer interaction with the contractor.

💬
Types of Messages Sent
Transactional only — no marketing

All messages sent through Wrench are transactional in nature and directly related to a job or service interaction. Message types include:

📋 Quote delivery 📅 Appointment reminders 🧾 Invoice delivery 💳 Payment follow-ups ⭐ Review requests 📢 Job status updates
Hi Sarah, Mike's Handyman Service sent you a quote for $285.00. Reply to this message with any questions or to confirm. Reply STOP to opt out.
Hi Sarah, reminder that Mike's Handyman Service is scheduled tomorrow at 9:00 AM at 123 Main St. Reply STOP to opt out.
Hi Sarah, your invoice from Mike's Handyman Service is ready — $285.00 due. Reply with questions or call Mike at (555) 000-0000. Reply STOP to opt out.
📊
Message Frequency
Job-based, not recurring

Message frequency varies based on the scope of the job. Customers typically receive 2–5 messages per service engagement (e.g., quote, appointment reminder, invoice). No recurring or scheduled promotional messages are sent.

🚫
Opt-Out & Help Instructions
Standard STOP / HELP support
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from all messages. You will receive a one-time confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
  • Reply HELP to receive support contact information for Wrench.
  • Opt-out instructions are included in all automated messages.
🔒
Data & Privacy
No data sold or shared

Phone numbers collected through Wrench are used solely to send transactional messages on behalf of the contractor. Phone numbers and message data are never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

For questions, contact us at support@getwrenchapp.com.
